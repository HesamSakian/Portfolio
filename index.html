About Hesam

Hesam is a skilled developer with over 10 years of professional experience across video games, digital media, HCI and immersive technologies (AR,VR,MR). He is also a lecturer on Immersive Technologies, Game Development and Programming. He believes in leveraging new technologies to transform dreams to reality! His passion is driven by his belief that XR, IoT & wearable tech alongside AI play a critical role in human evolution.

Over the past six years, I have immersed myself in projects at the intersection of technology, human perception, and interactive media, specializing in crafting compelling immersive experiences. My work bridges commercial, academic, and experimental domains, leveraging expertise in Unity3D, C#, and a diverse toolkit including Leap Motion, LIDAR, HTC Vive, Meta Quest, and Arduino. This technical foundation enables me to push boundaries and deliver innovative, user-driven interactions.

Projects

Meetex

Meetex is an immersive VR platform that reimagines digital interaction through the lens of Digital Twin theory, fostering intuitive and human-centric communication in virtual spaces. Serving as a foundational step toward the Metaverse, it combines two core environments—an interactive multi-floor exhibition hall and professional smart meeting spaces—where users engage via customizable avatars, spatial audio, and real-time emotes. With robust support for multimedia content, seamless connectivity, and AI-driven assistants, Meetex delivers a scalable, metaverse-ready experience optimized for VR headsets and tailored for exhibitions, conferences, and enterprise collaboration.
